Sanabarasingh

Sanabarasingh is famed for its vibrant ikat weaving, where skilled artisans craft sarees featuring intricate geometric and symbolic motifs, reflecting deep-rooted cultural traditions and exceptional handloom precision.

Maniabandh

Maniabandh is a historic weaving village in Odisha, known for its tie-dye techniques and Buddhist-inspired motifs. Artisans here specialize in crafting unique patterns with cultural and spiritual significance. Buddhism religion is practiced here.

Dhalapathar

Dhalapathar is renowned for its distinctive Dhalapathar Parda and handwoven bedsheets made from coarse cotton. Its traditional looms and weaving styles are central to the region's textile heritage. Single ikat is practiced here.
